Josh-Gudwin


5x Latin Grammy Awards, 3x Grammy Awards Josh Gudwin is a 3x US and 5x Latin Grammy Award-winning Mix Engineer, Record Producer, Vocal Producer and Musician. Josh Gudwin has become one of the most sought out mix engineers in the pop and Latin world with credits including record-breaking singles “Despacito” (the Justin Bieber Remix) “New Rules” & “Don’t Start Now” by Dua Lipa, as well as full albums projects Juanes Mis Planes Son Amarte, Bad Bunny X 100PRE & YHLQMDLG, J Balvin Vibras & Colores and the Balvin/Bunny collaborative album Oasis.

Heba Kadry
